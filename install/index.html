After installation:

1. Set your audio input

Open your app (Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord) and select VoiceChanger or CABLE Input as your microphone.

2. Launch the app

Run the start script. Open http://localhost:8766 in your browser.

3. Generate and capture

Type text, generate audio, click Capture — it plays through the virtual mic into your chat app.

Already have an access code?

Open the app, enter your access code, and start generating.
